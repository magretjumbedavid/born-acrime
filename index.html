The book is about Trevor and Patrical Who were staying in the , in this case Trevor was a son of Patricial . Trevor was born when there was apartheid. Trevor was a mixed child since his father was a white man and Patricial was black woman but they did not marry each other so Patricial raised Trevor as a single mother. The book was good. When I was reading it I was enjoying it because it has really encouraged me that I can do things that can be helpful to me regardless of what the community believes in. I can see Patricial doing things she was able to provide for her family yet her community believed that women's work is to do all the household work. Her behaviors allow Trevour to develop a hard working life because at some point Trevour was doing what his mother was doing, like working hard.
